About Me

I am a mechanical engineer, roboticist, web developer, and aspiring entrepreneur. I graduated from University of Wisconsin - Madison in May 2024 with a Bachelor's Degree in Mechanical Engineering. Since July 2024 I have been working with Amazon Robotics as a Deployment Robotics Engineer, where I travel the United States leading teams of engineers to retrofit, test, and install cutting edge robotics automation technology.

I am interested in Robotics and Mechanical Engineering, especially in automation, humaniod, and sensor applications. I have been working with robots for the past 7 years, starting my junior year of high school at Mercersburg Academy, where I founded the Blue Storm Robotics Club. Since then, I have been involved in numerous projects which have improved my skills as an engineer and inspired me to seek the unknown.
